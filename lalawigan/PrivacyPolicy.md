# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026

John Mark Delima ("we," "us," or "our") built the **Lalawigan** app as a free/ad-supported app. This strictly sets out our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our application and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using Lalawigan, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**Personal Data**
We do not require you to create an account, nor do we directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, physical address, or precise GPS location) to our servers.

**Local Storage (Game Data)**
Your personal game progress, including your guesses, game history, tutorial completion status, and current streaks, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this local data. If you uninstall the app or clear the app data, this localized progress will be lost.

**Global Statistics**
Lalawigan connects to the internet to fetch and update "Global Stats." The data transmitted for this feature is strictly limited to anonymous game statistics (e.g., win/loss records or number of guesses) to calculate global averages. This data cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

### 2. Third-Party Services and Analytics

While we do not collect personal information directly, Lalawigan uses **Google Analytics**, a third-party service, to help us understand how users interact with the app, monitor app performance, and fix bugs.

Google Analytics may automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information, such as:

* Device model and operating system version
* Anonymous usage data (e.g., screens visited, time spent in the app)
* Crash reports
* Approximate location (at the city or country level based on IP address)

This information is processed in aggregate and is used solely to improve the app's functionality and user experience.

For more information on how Google Analytics collects and processes data, please review their policy:

* [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. Security

We value your trust in using our app. Because your personal game data is stored exclusively on your local device, it is protected by your device's built-in operating system security. However, please remember that no method of electronic transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

### 4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. Because the app does not collect personal data directly, no such data is gathered by us. If we discover that a third-party service has inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to ensure it is deleted from those servers.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
